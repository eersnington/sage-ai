This Refund Policy outlines the terms under which Sage AI may provide refunds for our services.

## 1. Monthly Subscription Refunds

1.1 We do not typically offer refunds for our monthly subscription service. However, we may consider refunds on a case-by-case basis for exceptional circumstances.

## 2. Pay-Per-Report Refunds

2.1 For our pay-per-report option, we offer refunds within 24 hours of report generation if the report does not meet the described specifications due to a technical error on our part.

## 3. Refund Process

3.1 To request a refund, please contact us at sreeaadhi07@icloud.com with your account details and the reason for your refund request.

3.2 We will review your request and respond within 5 business days.

## 4. Exclusions

4.1 We do not offer refunds for:
   - Unused subscription time if you cancel your subscription mid-cycle
   - Reports that have been accessed or downloaded
   - Subscriptions that have been active for more than 7 days

## 5. Payment of Refunds

5.1 Approved refunds will be issued using the original method of payment.

## 6. Changes to This Policy

6.1 We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting on our website.

## 7. Contact Us

7.1 If you have any questions about this Refund Policy, please contact us at sreeaadhi07@icloud.com.
